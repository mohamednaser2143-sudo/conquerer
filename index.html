📖 How to Play

🎯 What's the goal?

Take over the entire world map by conquering every territory. Last player standing wins!

🗺️ The Map

The map is split into 38 territories across 6 continents. Each territory shows a colored circle with a number — that's how many armies are there. The color tells you who owns it.

🔄 What happens on my turn?

Every turn has 3 steps in order:

Step 1 — Deploy (place armies)
You get bonus armies at the start of your turn. The more territories you own, the more you get (minimum 3). Click on any territory YOU own to place one army there. Keep clicking until all your armies are placed. Then it moves to attack.

Step 2 — Attack (fight enemies)
This is optional — you can skip straight to fortify if you want.

To attack: Click one of YOUR territories that has 2 or more armies. Then click an ENEMY territory right next to it. Dice are rolled automatically:
• You roll up to 3 dice (depends on how many armies you have)
• The defender rolls up to 2 dice
• Highest dice are compared — if yours is higher, they lose an army. If tied or lower, you lose one.
• If the enemy territory hits 0 armies, you take it over!

You can keep attacking different territories or the same one. When you're done, click "Skip to Fortify".

Step 3 — Fortify (move armies)
Click one of your territories with 2+ armies, then click another one of YOUR territories right next to it. Armies will move over. This helps you strengthen your borders. Then click "End Turn" and the next player goes.

🌍 Continent Bonuses

If you own EVERY territory in a continent, you get extra armies each turn:

🟢 North America (9 territories) → +5 armies
🟢 South America (4 territories) → +2 armies
🟣 Europe (7 territories) → +5 armies
🟤 Africa (6 territories) → +3 armies
🔴 Asia (8 territories) → +7 armies
🔵 Australia (4 territories) → +2 armies

💀 Elimination

If a player loses all their territories, they're out. The attacker takes everything. Last player alive wins the game!

🤖 Playing vs Bots

Bots take their turns automatically — they deploy, attack weak neighbors, and move armies around. You just play your turns normally and watch them go.

💡 Tips

• Try to capture a small continent early (South America or Australia) for bonus armies.
• Don't spread your armies too thin — stack up on border territories.
• Sometimes it's smart to NOT attack and just build up your forces.
• Watch the player bar at the top to see who's getting too powerful.
